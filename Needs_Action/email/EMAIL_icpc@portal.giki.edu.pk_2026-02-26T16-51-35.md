Dear Team,

Your team was disqualified from the recent programming competition due to
one or more rule violations, including, but not limited to, plagiarism, the
use of unauthorized AI tools, or submission similarities, as detailed in
our internal review.

We are now offering your team an opportunity to appeal this decision. If
you wish to contest the disqualification, please submit a written appeal no
later than noon on 3 July 2025. Unfortunately, late submissions won't be
considered.

*Your appeal must:*

   - Clearly state your Team Name and University
   - Address the reason(s) for disqualification (refer to the
spreadsheet: Common
   Reasons for Disqualification
   <https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1NNIioSJtmY3qnXzt-oOFwetl3o8SCJ0nnXo9hvE8J7g/edit?usp=sharing>
   )
   - Could you provide any explanation or supporting evidence you deem
   relevant?



*Submit your appeal to icpc@portal.giki.edu.pk
<icpc@portal.giki.edu.pk> with the subject line:Appeal Submission – [Your
Team Name]*

The Appeal Resolution Committee will review all submissions. In some cases,
the committee may require further clarification via a Zoom call. If you are
contacted, please ensure your team is available for the discussion at the
scheduled time.

The final decision on your appeal will be made by 5 July 2025 (evening).

We would like you to please approach this process in a timely and
respectful manner.
